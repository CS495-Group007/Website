Group 007 - CS495 - Spring 2021

The goals of our project are to develop a fully functional web application that gameifies the practice of poetry scansion.
We want to deliver a fluid, seamless experience for students to study and for professors to monitor student progress. The mobile experience is our top priority, though we will need for the site to work well on laptops as well. The top priority is to make practicing scansion fun and simple.
Tools / APIs / Frameworks
  • React.JS Frontend
  • PhP + Laravel Backend
  • eTech Campus Hosting
  • MySQL DB

Student Experiences
  • Students can login to a personal account
  • Students can select Shakespearean Sonnets
  • Students can use color coding options to assign to sonnet blocks
  • Students can track their progress
  • Students can receive instructor feedback
Instructor Experiences
  • Instructors can import new sonnets
  • Instructors can assign sonnets to students
  • Instructors can monitor individual and class-wide progress

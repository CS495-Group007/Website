Group 007 - CS495 - Spring 2021

The goals of our project were to develop a fully functional web application that gameifies the practice of poetry scansion.
We placed heavy emphasis on delivering a fluid, seamless experience for guests, students, and instructors. Though various roadblocks ultimately kept us from completing the project's backend, we're proud of the progress we got to make.

  • PhP + Laravel Backend
